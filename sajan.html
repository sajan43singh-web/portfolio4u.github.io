About Me

Hi! I’m Sajan, an architecture student with a deep curiosity for how design can shape more sustainable, inclusive cities—especially in developing regions like Nepal. My work blends creativity with strategy, whether I’m exploring Transit-Oriented Development (TOD) solutions, crafting impactful presentations. I thrive on turning complex ideas into clear, engaging visuals and believe that thoughtful design can be a powerful tool for change!
