Project Overview

The Vehicle Station project is a modern transportation hub designed for efficiency, comfort, and accessibility. This project focuses on providing a seamless experience for travelers, integrating innovative architectural elements with practical functionality.

Key Features

  • Spacious waiting areas with natural lighting
  • Clear signage and easy navigation
  • Eco-friendly materials and energy-efficient design
  • Accessible facilities for all users
  • Modern ticketing and information systems

My Role

I was responsible for the overall design concept, 3D visualization, and user experience planning. My focus was on creating a welcoming and efficient space that meets the needs of both travelers and staff.
